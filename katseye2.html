#2: KATSEYE

KASTEYE, pronounced as "cat's eye"; is a global girl group formed through the survival show “Dream Academy” and managed by
HYBE LABELS X Geffen Records. The members consists of (Left to Right): Daniela, Sophia, Manon, Megan, Yoonchae, and Lara.
KATSEYE debuted in June 28, 2024 with the Single: Debut.
