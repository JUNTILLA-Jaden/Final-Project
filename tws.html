#1: TWS

TWS, pronounced as "Two-us"; an abbreviation of Twenty Four Seven With US
is a south korean group formed and managed by PLEDIS ENTERTAINMENT.
The members consists of (Left to Right): Kyungmin, Dohoon, Hanjin, Jihoon, Shinyu, and Youngjae.
TWS debuted in January 22, 2024 with the Extended Play: Sparkling Blue.
